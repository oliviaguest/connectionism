 \begin{columns}[T]
    \begin{column}{.45\textwidth} 
     \ \\ 
     \ \\

 \item A set of \emph{units}, connected by \emph{weights} \\ \
\item The network \emph{runs} by passing \emph{activations} from the \emph{input} (to the \emph{hidden}) to the \emph{output} units \\ \
